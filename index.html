Terms of Service summary

PredatorGuard access is limited to authenticated members. You are responsible for lawful use, truthful account data, protecting your credentials, and following safety and moderation workflows. Access can be suspended for abuse, unsafe conduct, or policy violations.

Privacy Notice summary

The app stores account profile data in Supabase, uses an HTTP-only session cookie for sign-in, and only stores browser location after you opt in. Device coordinates stay on this browser right now, while your location-sharing preference and legal acceptance timestamps are saved with your profile.
